A bit about us

The Charter School of Wilmington is one of 3 high schools in the world to posses an offical ACM Chapter. This is not just some ordinary computer club, but an extension of one of the most prestigious computer research organizations in the world.

Here you will be able to find links to our repositories and curriculm resources we have created. Feel free to take a look around!

Areas of interest and lesson topics

Web Design

The web is all around us. Just about everyone has access to the internet, and without web design there wouldn't be any sites to look at! We teach not just web development techniques and languages (such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, etc.) but also design principles. This gives members a comprehensive view of how to design and develop websites.

Networking

Server infrastructure is a very crucial area within computer science. Networking allows everything from text messages to the internet to work. However, it is often hard for people to learn networking on their own. ACM plans to help resolve this and give people a chance to learn the basics of networking and how to operate and manage Linux servers.

Artificial Intelligence

AI is a rapidly expanding field in computer science, and there is no way we couldn't cover it! AI is a new area of focus for CSW ACM and we have a variety of cool lessons planned! Such as how to use TensorFlow and how the code for AI works behind the scene.
